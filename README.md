# Trzech Tragicznych Turystów (Three Terrible Tourists) (TTT[^1])

This will be a multiplayer party game made for [Campfire Lublin](https://campfire.hackclub.com/lublin) and [Ember](https://ember.hackclub.com/) by the trzeh team.

### Short story:
During your vacation, you and your friends saw an elevator and decided to enter it. It took you underground, and it appears that the elevator is cursed... Now you are forced to compete with your friends to say alive.
### Game status:
Currently, the game is in an early prototype/demo status (see [the demo branch](https://github.com/trzeh/ttt-godot/tree/demo)). It only supports singleplayer, has some visual bugs and only a few minigames. Nonetheless, you can try it and give us feedback! (see [below for itch.io link](#itch.io-link))
The game will be updated further for Campfire Ember.
